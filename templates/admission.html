Admission Guidelines

Our school welcomes students from diverse backgrounds. Please review the guidelines and requirements for different grades.

Age Requirements:

For L.K.G: 3 years 6 months as on June 1st of the year of admission.

Documents Required:

  • Birth Certificate
  • Aadhar Card
  • Previous School's TC (if applicable)

Class 2
Building on foundational skills and introducing new concepts.
Class 3
Emphasis on reading, writing, and arithmetic.
Class 4
Enhancement of critical thinking and problem-solving skills.
Class 5
Introduction to more complex subjects like science and social studies.
Class 6
Transition to middle school with increased focus on academics.
Class 7
Development of analytical and critical thinking skills.
Class 8
Preparation for higher-level subjects and increased responsibility.
Class 9
Introduction to advanced topics and specialization.
Class 10
Comprehensive preparation for board exams and future studies.
